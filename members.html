Wayne Sermon-guitarist

Wayne Sermon is the lead guitarist for the Grammy-winning rock band Imagine Dragons, known for his distinctive guitar work and contributions to the band's signature sound. He joined the band in 2009 and has been integral to hits like "Radioactive" and "Demons."

Dan Reynolds-singer

Dan Reynolds is the lead vocalist and primary songwriter for rock band Imagine Dragons. Known for his powerful voice and energetic stage presence, he has helped the band achieve global success with hits like "Radioactive" and "Believer."

Daniel Platzman-drummer

Daniel James Platzman is an American musician, songwriter, record producer and composer. He is best known as the former drummer for the pop rock band Imagine Dragons."
