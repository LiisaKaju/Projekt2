A little about us

History

Imagine Dragons is an American rock band formed in 2008 in Las Vegas, known for their fusion of rock, pop, and electronic music.

Awards

Imagine Dragons has been a force to be reckoned with in the music industry, racking up numerous accolades that reflect their impact and artistry. The band has clinched multiple Grammy Awards, including Best Rock Performance for their breakthrough hit "Radioactive." Their trophy case also boasts several American Music Awards, Billboard Music Awards, and MTV Music Awards, showcasing their widespread appeal and undeniable talent. Their innovative sound and powerful performances continue to earn them recognition from critics and fans alike, solidifying Imagine Dragons as one of the defining rock bands of the modern era.
